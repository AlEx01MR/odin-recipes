Parmesan Fries

Savoury parmesan fries with garlic and parsley

Ingredients

  • 500g - Frozen french fries
  • 150g - Parmesan cheese or your choice of hard cheese (Grana Padano, Pecorino Romano, etc)
  • Half a cloth - Garlic (lots of it)
  • One root - Parsley

Steps

  1. Put the frozen fries into the hot oil (around 270 degrees Celcius)
  2. Start peeling the garlic and use a garlic press to make it into a pasta
  3. Start grating your choice of cheese (always use cheese blocks, not pregrated cheese)
  4. Cut your parsley
  5. Prepare some fresh pepper
  6. Take out your fries and put them onto some paper towels to draw the excess oil
  7. Put your fries into the plate. Season with salt and fresh cracked pepper. Put around 30% of your parmesan cheese first, followed by all of your garlic, rest of your parmesan and finish with the parsley

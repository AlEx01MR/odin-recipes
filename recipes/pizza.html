Pizza

Incredible thicc and savoury Detroit style pizza

Ingredients

      Dough

    • 1kg of white flour
    • 21g of active yeast
    • 30g of salt
    • 650ml luke warm water

      Toppings

    • 2 onions
    • 500g tomato sauce
    • 2 hot peppers
    • 500g canned mushrooms
    • 400g cheese
    • 200g chorizzo

Steps

      Dough

    1. Put the flour, salt and yeast in your mixing bowl
    2. Start adding watter slowly
    3. After finishing adding the watter keep kneading or mixing for 10 minutes
    4. Let the dough rest for 1 hour

      Toppings

    1. Cut your onion in rings
    2. Cut your peppers in rings
    3. Drain your mushrooms
    4. Unpack your tomato sauce and add basil if you want
    5. Grate your cheese

      Assembly

    1. This recipe is for 2 pizzas, so divide your dough into 2 equal pieces
    2. Lightly flower your pan
    3. Stretch your dough into the pan
    4. Add your tomato sauce, onion, hot pepper and mushrooms
    5. Put in oven for 10 minutes at max heat
    6. After 10 minutes get the pizza out and add the chorrizo and the grated cheese
    7. Put it back in the over for another 3-5 minutes
    8. Take it out, let it rest for 5 minutes and enjoy!
